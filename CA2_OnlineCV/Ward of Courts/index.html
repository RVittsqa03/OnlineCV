Background

This website is based on a true life situation. When a petition to the Wards of Court is made, the said person is known as "the Ward". The Wards of Court 'looks after the Ward's welfare and assets' and does not have to include the Ward or his family in any meetings or say. The process of the system is not transparent and needs an overhaul review. If you are considering going down this route you should heed some facts that few will share within their professions.
